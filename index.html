JOIN US

Isica is irrevocably shaped by her past and you play a crucial role in her future. Create a unique character, decide how you want their story to evolve, and discover new insights, questions, and truths with each decision you make.

TRAILER

The story of Great Cast War begins when you create your first character. You can choose from several biographical characteristics that reflect his personality, his history, his preferences and his values. This biography will determine the path of your story and your future decisions will make it go in different directions.

Your character's race determines which of the three great cities he will have his home in and where you start to play. Your personal story will guide you through chapters in instances that show your character's life and adventures as they shape their own destiny. If you need help, you can always invite your friends to join you as “guest stars,” but you will be the one to make the key decisions.
