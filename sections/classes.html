ROGUE

Life is an endless adventure for those who live by their ingenuity. Always one step ahead of danger, Rogues rely on their cunning, skills, and charisma to force luck in their favor. Never knowing what to expect, they are prepared for anything, mastering a wide variety of skills, training themselves as master manipulators, agile acrobats, shadow stalkers, or master of any of dozens of other professions or talents. Thieves and gamblers, liars and diplomats, bandits and bounty hunters, scouts and investigators - all can be considered rogues, as well as countless other professions that rely on wits, skill, or luck. Although most rogues prefer cities and the myriad opportunities of civilization, some embrace life on the road, traveling far, meeting exotic people, and encountering fantastic dangers in search of equally fantastic riches. In the end, anyone who wishes to shape his destiny and live life on his own terms could be called a rogue.

PAPER

Rogues excel by moving invisibly and capturing their unsuspecting enemies and tend to avoid close combat. Their varied abilities allow them to be highly versatile, with great variations in technical knowledge among thieves. Most, however, excel at overcoming obstacles of all kinds, from opening doors and disarming traps to outwitting magical dangers and ripping off stupid opponents.
