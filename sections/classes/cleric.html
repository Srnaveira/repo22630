CLERIC

In faith and divine miracles, many can find a greater purpose. Called to serve powers beyond mortal understanding, all priests preach wonders and meet the spiritual needs of their people. Clergymen are more than just priests, however; These emissaries of divinity do the will of their deities through the force of arms and the magic of their gods. Devoted to the principles of the religions and philosophies that inspire them, these ecclesiastics seek to extend the knowledge and influence of their faith. Although they may share similar abilities, clerics are as different from one another as the divinities they serve, some offering healing and redemption, others judging law and truth, and others spreading conflict and corruption. The paths of the clerics are varied, although all those who follow these paths walk with the most powerful ally and carry the weapons of the gods themselves.

PAPER

More than capable of defending the honor of their gods in battle, clerics often make for robust and capable combatants. His true strength lies in his ability to harness the power of his gods, whether from increasing his own value and that of his allies in battle, to irritating his enemies with divine magic, or to provide healing to companions in need.
