WIZARD

Beyond the veil of the mundane are hidden the secrets of absolute power. The works of beings beyond mortals, the legends of realms where gods and spirits walk, the lore of wonderful and terrible creations, such mysteries call those with ambition and intellect to rise above ordinary people to achieve true power. That is the way of the magician. These skilled users of magic seek, collect, and covet esoteric lore, turning to the occult arts to create wonders beyond the abilities of mere mortals. While some choose a particular field of magical study and become masters of those powers, others embrace versatility, reveling in the untethered wonders of all magic. In any case, wizards display an ingenious and potent array of abilities, capable of punishing their enemies, empowering their allies, and shaping the world as they wish.

PAPER

While universal wizards could study to prepare for any kind of danger, specialist wizards look for schools of magic that make them specially qualified within a specific focus. However, regardless of their specialty, all magicians are masters of the impossible and can assist their allies in overcoming any danger.
