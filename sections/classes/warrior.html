WARRIOR

Some take up arms for glory, wealth, or revenge. Others struggle to prove themselves, protect others, or because they don't know how to do anything else. Still others learn the ways of weapons to hone their bodies in battle and prove themselves in the forging of war. Lords of the battlefield, warriors are very diverse, training with many weapons or just one, perfecting the uses of armor, learning fighting techniques from exotic masters, and studying the art of combat, all to become weapons themselves. living. Much more than mere thugs, these skilled warriors reveal the deadly truth of their weapons, turning bits of metal into weapons capable of taming kingdoms, slaughtering monsters, and exalting the hearts of armies. Soldiers, knights, hunters, and artists of war, warriors are unparalleled champions, and woe to those who dare to rise up against them!

PAPER

Warriors excel in combat by defeating their enemies, controlling the flow of battle, and surviving raids. While their specific weapons and methods grant them a wide variety of tactics, few can match warriors in sheer battle prowess.
