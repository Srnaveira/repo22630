NEWS

New ORC race arrive.

coming soon: Along with their brute strength and comparatively low intellect, the main difference between orcs and civilized humanoids is their attitude. As a culture, orcs are violent and aggressive, with the strongest dominating the rest through fear and brutality.

They take what they want by force, and they don't mind murdering or enslaving entire towns when they can get away with it. They don't have time for niceties or details, and their camps and towns tend to be grubby, with ramshackle businesses filled with drunken fights, pit fighting, and other sadistic entertainments.

Lacking the patience for agriculture and being able to herd only the most robust and self-sufficient animals, orcs almost always find it easier to take what someone else has built rather than create it themselves.
They are arrogant and easy to anger in the face of a challenge, but they only care about honor to the extent that it directly benefits them to do so.

New DRUID class arriving in december

coming soon: In the purity of the elements and the order of nature there is a power beyond the wonders of civilization. Stealthy, yet undeniable, this primal magic is guarded by the servants of philosophical balance known as druids.

Allied with the beasts and manipulators of nature, these often misunderstood protectors of the wild strive to shield their lands from those who would threaten them and demonstrate the power of nature to those who lock themselves behind city walls.

Rewarded for their devotion with incredible powers, Druids gain unparalleled shapeshifting abilities, an understanding of mighty beasts, and the power to call forth the fury of nature.
The most powerful wield powers such as storms, earthquakes and volcanoes with primal wisdom long abandoned and forgotten by civilization.
